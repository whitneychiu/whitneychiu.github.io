Huei-Chen Whitney Chiu

I am a second year Master student in Biostatistics at the University of Toronto. I am currently a research assistant at the Hospital of Sick Children (SickKids). My research interests lie in health economics, pharmaco-epidemiology and related research methods with an emphasis on evaluating health interventions and better understanding the intervention timing through novel algorithms. I am also motivated to inform healthcare policy decisions with evidence generated from real-world data.
